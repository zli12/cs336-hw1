Lily found a map in her attic and decided to follow it into the deep forest. As she walked, she saw a small tree with a big hole in it. Lily thought, "I will hide in the hole and wait for the old man."
Lily found a long stick and used it to reach into the hole. The old man was very happy with his map. He thought, "Thank you, Lily!" The old man was happy too because he could help Lily. They both stayed friends and had a fun day in the big green forest.
