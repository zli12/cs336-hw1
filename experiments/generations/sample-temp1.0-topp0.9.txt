Once upon a time, there was a little boy named Tim. Tim loved to play outside in the wild. One day, Tim's mom asked him to behave. She said, "Tim, please behave, I can't play outside today."
Tim was sad because he really wanted to play outside. He went outside to find a new place to play. He saw a big ball and thought, "I will play catch and get the ball from my mom."
Tim ran to his mom and said, "Mom, let's play catch. I am very good at it." His mom agreed and they played catch together. Tim was happy because he behaved and did not get punished.
