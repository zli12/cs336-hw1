Once upon a time, there was a little girl named Lily. She lived in a small house near the woods. Every day, Lily liked to play outside with her friends.
One day, Lily found a fragile egg. It was small and scared. She wanted to protect it, but her friend Tim said, "No, Lily! The egg is fragile. It might break." Lily was sad, but she understood.
Lily kept her fragile egg safe. One day, she saw a big, beautiful butterfly. She was so happy! She watched the butterfly as it flew around her neck. Then, she saw a big tree. Lily had an idea. She would put the butterfly back in the tree. She knew it would keep her fragile egg safe. And so, she took care of it.
