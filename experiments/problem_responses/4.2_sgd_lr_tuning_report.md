# 4.2 SGD Learning Rate Tuning

Figure: `4.2_sgd_lr_tuning_plot.svg` plots training loss vs. iteration (10 steps) for the toy SGD example at learning rates `1e1`, `1e2`, and `1e3`, with the y-axis shown on a log scale. The log scale makes it easy to compare both fast decay (`1e2`) and rapid explosion (`1e3`) on the same chart.

Observed behavior: with `lr=1e1`, loss decreases steadily and remains stable; with `lr=1e2`, loss decreases much faster and quickly reaches near zero in this run; with `lr=1e3`, loss diverges and grows dramatically over iterations.
