Once upon a time, there was a little boy named Tim. He loved to play outside with his toys. One day, he went to the park with his mom. At the park, he found a big slide. Tim was very happy and started to slide down. He laughed and smiled as he went up and down.
As Tim was sliding, he saw a man with a fake gun. The man looked at Tim's gun and thought it was cool. He was brave and wanted to try it. Tim got off the swing and walked to the man. He said, "Hi, I'm Tim. What is your name?" The man smiled and said, "Hi Tim, I'm Ben."
Tim and Ben became friends. They played together in the park all day. They shared their toys and had fun. Tim was glad he didn't hurt the fake gun. He was happy to have a new friend in Ben. And they all lived happily ever after.
