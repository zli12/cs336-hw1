Once upon a time, there was a little boy named Tim. Tim loved to play with his toy cars. One day, Tim found a big, heavy rock in his room. He thought it was a fun toy.
Tim's mom saw him with the heavy rock. She said, "Tim, that rock is too heavy for you to play with. You will get hurt if you touch it." Tim did not want to get hurt, so he listened to his mom.
Tim played with his heavy rock all day. He was very happy. At night, he put the rock under his bed. Tim went to sleep with a big smile on his face.
