Once upon a time, there was a little girl named Lucy. She was very creative. She liked to draw and paint all day. One day, Lucy was playing with her friends in the park.
While playing, Lucy saw a little boy named Tim. Tim was sad because he had no toys to play with. Lucy wanted to help Tim. She said, "I will lend you my toys if you let me play with you." Tim was happy and said, "Thank you, Lucy!"
Lucy and Tim played together all day. They had so much fun. They became best friends. Lucy learned that sharing and lending can bring good things to others. And Tim learned that being creative and sharing made him happy too.
