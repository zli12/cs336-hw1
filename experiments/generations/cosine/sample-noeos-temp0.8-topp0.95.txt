Once upon a time, there was a little boy named Tim. Tim had a red bucket. He liked to play with his bucket in the park. One day, Tim met a new friend named Sue. Sue saw Tim's red bucket and asked, "Can I play with your bucket?"
Tim said, "Yes, you can play with my bucket. But be careful, it's my favorite bucket." Sue was very happy and they played together. They filled the bucket with sand and made castles. They had a lot of fun.
After playing, Tim and Sue were tired. They sat down under a big tree. Tim used his red bucket to cover his eyes. He said, "Thank you, Sue, for letting me play with your bucket. I had a great time." Sue smiled and said, "I like your red bucket too, Tim." They both knew they would have many more fun days at the park.
