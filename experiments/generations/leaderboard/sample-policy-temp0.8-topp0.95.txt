The president said today that the country must focus on re-establishing local communities, something he called “the American Dream.”

During a call with reporters Monday, the president said he recognizes the importance of developing local communities, but “the loss of our local communities would be catastrophic.”

“So why does it matter now? I don’t know, but I’ve said it before and I’ll say it again: American cities need to be transformed,” the president said. “And I believe we need to do that.”

He added that “the loss of our local communities would be catastrophic.”
